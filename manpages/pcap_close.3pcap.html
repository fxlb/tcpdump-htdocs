NAME

pcap_close - close a capture device or savefile

SYNOPSIS

#include <pcap/pcap.h>

void pcap_close(pcap_t *p);

DESCRIPTION

pcap_close() closes the files associated with p and deallocates resources. This means that, for example, if p refers to a savefile that was opened with pcap_fopen_offline() or pcap_fopen_offline_with_tstamp_precision(), the stream provided to the routine will be closed unless it is stdin.
