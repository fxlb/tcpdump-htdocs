LINKTYPE_BLUETOOTH_HCI_H4_WITH_PHDR

Packet structure

+---------------------------+
|         Direction         |
|         (4 Octets)        |
+---------------------------+
|   HCI packet indicator    |
|         (1 Octet)         |
+---------------------------+
|          Payload          |
.                           .
.                           .
.                           .

Description

The Direction field is a 4-octet direction field, in big-endian byte order, the low-order bit of which is set if the frame was sent from the host to the controller and clear if the frame was received by the host from the controller.

The HCI packet indicator field contains a packet indicator value, as specified by Volume 4, Part A, Section 2 "Protocol" of the Bluetooth Core specification.

The payload is an HCI packet, as specified by Volume 4, Part E "Host Controller Interface Functional Specification" of the Bluetooth Core specification, of the packet type indicated by the packet indicator value.
