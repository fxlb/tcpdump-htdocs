LINKTYPE_ZWAVE_TAP

Packet structure

+--------------------------+
| Exegin Z-Wave tap header |
|    (Variable Length)     |
+--------------------------+
|       Z-Wave MPDU        |
.                          .
.                          .
.                          .

Description

The Z-Wave tap header is specified by the Z-Wave G.9959 TAP Link Type Specification.

The Z-Wave MPDU is specified by section 8.1.3 "MPDU format" of G.9959.
