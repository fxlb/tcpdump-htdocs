LINKTYPE_DSA_TAG_BRCM

Packet structure for Ethernet frames with a Broadcom switch tag inserted

+--------------------------------+
| Destination Address (6 octets) |
+--------------------------------+
|    Source Address (6 octets)   |
+--------------------------------+
| Broadcom switch tag (4 octets) |
+--------------------------------+
|     Type/Length (2 octets)     |
+--------------------------------+
|            Payload             |
.                                .
.                                .
.                                .
                
Description



A Broadcom switch tag
is inserted in the Ethernet header before the Type/Length field.
