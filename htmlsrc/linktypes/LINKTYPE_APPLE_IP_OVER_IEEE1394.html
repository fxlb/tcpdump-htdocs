LINKTYPE_APPLE_IP_OVER_IEEE1394

Packet structure

+---------------------+
| Destination address |
|      (8 Octets)     |
+---------------------+
|    Source address   |
|      (8 Octets)     |
+---------------------+
|     Packet type     |
|      (2 Octets)     |
+---------------------+
|       Payload       |
.                     .
.                     .
.                     .

Description

The destination and source addresses are the EUI-64 IEEE 1394 addresses of the destination and source of the packet. The packet type is an Ethernet type, in network byte order.
