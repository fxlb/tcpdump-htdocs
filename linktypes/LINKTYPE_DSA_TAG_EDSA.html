LINKTYPE_DSA_TAG_EDSA

Packet structure for Ethernet frames with a Marvell EDSA switch tag inserted

Switch tag structure

+------------------------------------+
|   Destination Address (6 octets)   |
+------------------------------------+
|      Source Address (6 octets)     |
+------------------------------------+
| Marvell EDSA switch tag (8 octets) |
+------------------------------------+
|       Type/Length (2 octets)       |
+------------------------------------+
|              Payload               |
.                                    .
.                                    .
.                                    .

Description

A Marvell EDSA switch tag is inserted in the Ethernet header before the Type/Length field.
