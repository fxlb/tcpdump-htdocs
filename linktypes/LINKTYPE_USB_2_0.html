LINKTYPE_USB_2_0

Description

USB 2.0, 1.1, or 1.0 packets, beginning with a PID, as described by Chapter 8 "Protocol Layer" of the the Universal Serial Bus Specification Revision 2.0. Deprecated in favor of speed specific USB 2.0, 1.1, or 1.0 linktypes.
